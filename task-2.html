Tribute to DR.B.R.Ambedkar

Tribute to Dr BR Ambedkar-saheb on his death anniversary

On 6 December 1956, India lost a great soul and a legendary figure who passed away at his home in Delhi, he was none other than Dr Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar, (Dr B.R. Ambedkar) popularly known as Babasaheb/Ambedkar-saheb. Dr Babasaheb was born on 14 April 1891; the day in many parts of the country is celebrated as New Year. Time to time many genius persons were born in India and all were not born with a silver spoon as many of them had to struggle in their life owing to economic, social and other conditions. Dr B.R. Ambedkar was one such person who had to fight in his life because of social stigma, not economic. With his knowledge, wisdom and humanitarian feelings he had proved by caste one cannot be great and one can be great only by deeds. He was jurist, economist, politician, social reformer, prolific writer and sociologist as a sequel he could reach to the zenith of intellectual excellence. Also, he was the architect of our constitution for which we all are proud as almost same time Pakistan was created but within 25 years East Pakistan became Bangladesh and several times Army rule was observed in Pakistan.
